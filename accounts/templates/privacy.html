Important Notice

At Cauxie Sales, we value your privacy. We do not store any personal data from our customers. All transactions are handled directly through WhatsApp and Discord communications.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cauxie Sales ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our services. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your information.

Please read this policy carefully. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined here.

2. Information We Collect

Minimal Information

We collect minimal information necessary to process your orders:

  • Email address (for order notifications only)
  • Communication history via WhatsApp or Discord

What We Don't Collect:

  • We do not store personal data
  • We do not use payment processors that store your financial information
  • We do not use tracking tools like Google Analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders for game accounts
  • Sending order confirmations and notifications
  • Providing customer support via WhatsApp or Discord
  • Sending promotional messages (only with your consent)

4. Data Security

Since we do not store personal data, your information remains secure through these measures:

  • All transactions are handled through secure WhatsApp and Discord communications
  • No databases storing customer information
  • No retention of customer data beyond immediate transaction needs

5. Marketing Communications

We may occasionally send marketing emails about new products, special offers, or other information we think you may find interesting.

Opting Out:

You can easily opt out of marketing communications at any time by messaging us on WhatsApp and requesting to be removed from our mailing list.

6. Third-Party Services

We value your privacy and have intentionally limited our use of third-party services:

  • We do not use any third-party payment processors
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share your information with any third parties

7. Data Retention

We do not retain customer data. Once a transaction is completed, we do not store any personal information. Communication history on WhatsApp or Discord is subject to those platforms' privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
